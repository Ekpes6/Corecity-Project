### Agent & Seller Terms of Engagement

**Effective Date:** May 14, 2026
**Platform:** CoreCity Properties

## PREAMBLE

This Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between **CoreCity Properties** ("the Platform", "CoreCity") and each registered user operating in the capacity of a **Licensed Agent** or **Property Seller** ("the Party") upon acceptance of the terms contained herein.

By registering, listing a property, or engaging in any transaction facilitated by CoreCity Properties, the Party acknowledges that they have read, understood, and agree to be legally bound by the provisions of this Agreement.

## PART II — AGENT TERMS

### 2.1 Role Definition

A **Licensed Agent** is a professional intermediary who:
- Lists properties on the Platform on behalf of property owners
- Manages the full lifecycle of listed properties including viewings, negotiations, and reservation handling
- Acts in the interest of both the property owner and the Platform's buyer/tenant community

### 2.2 Eligibility

To operate as an Agent on CoreCity, the Party must:
1. Hold a valid real estate licence or professional certification recognised in their jurisdiction
2. Provide accurate identification and contact information at the point of registration
3. Maintain an active and verified account on the Platform
4. Agree to the Platform's Know Your Customer (KYC) requirements as may be updated from time to time

### 2.3 Agent Obligations

The Agent agrees to:

1. **Accuracy of Listings** — Ensure all property details, descriptions, images, pricing, and owner information submitted to the Platform are accurate, current, and not misleading
2. **Owner Authorisation** — Obtain express written consent from the property owner before listing any property; CoreCity shall not be liable for disputes arising from unauthorised listings
3. **Owner Contact Details** — Provide the owner's full name, phone number, bank name, account number, and account name at the time of listing to facilitate disbursement processing
4. **Timely Communication** — Respond to reservation requests, buyer enquiries, and Platform notifications within 48 hours
5. **Confidentiality** — Not disclose buyer, tenant, or owner personal data obtained through the Platform to any third party without prior consent
6. **Compliance** — Abide by all applicable real estate laws, anti-money laundering (AML) regulations, and consumer protection statutes in their operating jurisdiction
7. **Conflict of Interest** — Disclose any personal or financial relationship with the property owner or prospective buyer/tenant that may affect impartiality

### 2.4 Agent Commission Structure

| Component | Rate | Payout Method |
|---|---|---|
| **Agent Commission** | **7%** of Property Value | Credited to Platform Wallet |
| **CoreCity Platform Fee** | **3%** of Property Value | Retained by CoreCity |
| **Total** | **10%** of Property Value | — |

#### 2.4.1 Payment Conditions
- Commission is earned only upon a Transaction being marked as **fully completed** by the Platform
- The Agent's 7% is credited to their Platform Wallet upon transaction completion
- Wallet balances may be withdrawn by the Agent subject to the Platform's withdrawal policy (see Part IV)
- CoreCity reserves the right to withhold commission if the Agent is found to be in breach of any provision of this Agreement

#### 2.4.2 No Commission Events
No commission shall be payable where:
- A Transaction is cancelled before completion due to the Agent's negligence or misconduct
- The property listing is found to be fraudulent or materially misrepresented
- The Agent fails to meet KYC requirements at the time of disbursement

## PART III — SELLER TERMS

### 3.1 Role Definition

A **Property Seller** is a property owner who:
- Lists their own properties directly on the Platform without a third-party agent
- Manages their listings, responds to enquiries, and interfaces directly with buyers or tenants
- Receives disbursements directly to their registered bank account

### 3.2 Eligibility

To operate as a Seller on CoreCity, the Party must:
1. Be the legal owner (or authorised representative) of any property listed
2. Provide valid proof of ownership upon request by the Platform
3. Provide accurate personal identification, phone number, and bank account details for disbursement
4. Agree to the Platform's KYC requirements

### 3.3 Seller Obligations

The Seller agrees to:

1. **Proof of Ownership** — Maintain and provide, upon Platform request, valid documentation confirming legal ownership of every listed property
2. **Accurate Representation** — Ensure all listing information including price, size, amenities, location, and images is truthful and current
3. **Bank Account Accuracy** — Provide and maintain correct bank name, account number, and account name to ensure accurate disbursement; CoreCity shall not be liable for misdirected payments due to incorrect details
4. **Reservation Honouring** — Honour all confirmed reservations and not privately renegotiate transaction terms outside the Platform
5. **Availability** — Be reasonably reachable at the phone number registered on the Platform during active listing periods
6. **Compliance** — Comply with all applicable property laws, tax obligations, and consumer protection regulations
7. **Non-Circumvention** — Not solicit buyers or tenants identified through the Platform to complete transactions outside the Platform in order to avoid Platform fees

### 3.4 Seller Commission Structure

| Component | Rate | Payout Method |
|---|---|---|
| **Seller Disbursement Bonus** | **5%** of Property Value | Bank Transfer (direct payout) |
| **CoreCity Platform Fee** | **5%** of Property Value | Retained by CoreCity |
| **Total** | **10%** of Property Value | — |

#### 3.4.1 Payment Conditions
- The Seller's 5% bonus is disbursed via direct bank transfer upon the Platform marking the Transaction as complete
- Disbursement processing may take **3–7 business days** depending on the Seller's financial institution
- The Seller must ensure their registered bank account details are accurate; reprocessing fees for failed transfers due to incorrect details shall be borne by the Seller
- CoreCity reserves the right to withhold disbursement pending verification if a Transaction is flagged for review

#### 3.4.2 No Disbursement Events
No disbursement shall be made where:
- The property listing is found to be fraudulent or in breach of ownership claims
- The Transaction is reversed or voided due to Seller-side breach
- The Seller's bank account details are unverifiable or rejected by the financial institution after two attempts

## PART IV — WITHDRAWALS AND DISBURSEMENTS

### 4.1 Agent Withdrawals

1. Agents may submit withdrawal requests for their Platform Wallet balance at any time through the Dashboard
2. Withdrawal requests are subject to Platform review and shall be processed within **5 business days**
3. A minimum withdrawal threshold of **₦5,000** (or equivalent) applies
4. CoreCity reserves the right to place a temporary hold on withdrawal requests pending transaction settlement confirmation

### 4.2 Seller Disbursements

1. Seller disbursements are initiated by the Platform Admin upon confirmation of transaction completion
2. The Seller will receive a notification confirming disbursement initiation
3. Sellers must not request disbursement before the buyer's payment has been confirmed and cleared on the Platform

### 4.3 Dispute on Disbursement

Any dispute regarding disbursement amounts must be raised in writing to **support@corecity.properties** within **14 days** of the disbursement date. Disputes raised after this window may not be entertained.

## PART V — PROPERTY LISTING STANDARDS

Both Agents and Sellers must ensure all listings meet the following standards:

1. **Images** — A minimum of three (3) clear, current photographs of the property must be uploaded. AI-generated or stock images are prohibited
2. **Pricing** — Pricing must reflect genuine market rates. Artificially inflated prices for commission manipulation are prohibited
3. **Location** — State and LGA must be accurately selected. The full property address shall be disclosed to confirmed reservation holders only
4. **Amenities** — Only amenities physically present and operational at the property may be listed
5. **Property Type** — The correct property type (residential, commercial, etc.) and listing type (for sale, for rent) must be selected

CoreCity reserves the right to delist, suspend, or permanently remove any property listing that violates these standards, without prior notice.

## PART VI — PLATFORM CONDUCT AND PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES

All registered Parties are strictly prohibited from:

1. **Fraud** — Submitting false property information, fabricating ownership documents, or impersonating property owners
2. **Fee Avoidance** — Directing buyers or tenants to transact outside the Platform to circumvent Platform fees
3. **Data Misuse** — Using buyer, tenant, or owner data obtained via the Platform for purposes beyond the facilitation of the specific transaction
4. **Misrepresentation** — Falsely claiming credentials, licences, or property ownership
5. **Harassment** — Harassing, threatening, or engaging in discriminatory conduct towards buyers, tenants, or Platform staff
6. **Manipulation** — Artificially inflating property views, reservation counts, or transaction values to distort commissions

Breach of any of the above shall result in immediate account suspension, commission forfeiture, and may be reported to relevant regulatory authorities.

1. CoreCity acts as a **facilitating marketplace** and is not a party to any transaction between the Seller/Agent and the buyer/tenant
2. CoreCity makes no warranty as to the accuracy of any listing published by an Agent or Seller and shall not be held liable for buyer or tenant losses arising from misrepresentation by the listing Party
3. CoreCity shall not be liable for delays in disbursement or withdrawal processing caused by banking system failures, third-party payment processor issues, or regulatory holds outside the Platform's control
4. The Platform's total liability to any registered Party in connection with any claim shall not exceed the total commissions earned by that Party in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim

## PART VIII — SUSPENSION AND TERMINATION

### 8.1 By CoreCity

CoreCity may suspend or terminate a Party's account with or without notice for:
- Breach of any provision of this Agreement
- Suspected fraudulent activity pending investigation
- Regulatory requirement or court order
- Prolonged inactivity (>12 months with no active listings)

Upon termination, any pending commission or disbursement due to the Party at the point of termination shall be settled in accordance with the Platform's standard processing timelines, unless the termination is for cause (fraud, misconduct), in which case CoreCity reserves the right to withhold pending funds pending investigation.

### 8.2 By the Party

A Party may deactivate their account at any time by submitting a written request to CoreCity. Outstanding obligations (active listings, confirmed reservations) must be resolved before deactivation is processed.
